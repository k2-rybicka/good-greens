About

We are a non-profit organisation based in Bristol. Our restaurant works on "pay as you feel" basis. Using diverted fresh food and other foodstuffs from local supermarkets and distributors, we are trying to minimise food waste and help those in need.

Alongside the restaurant, we also have a food warehouse. You can buy a subscription to collect produce boxes with fresh ingredients!

Pay As You Feel

Pay As You Feel means there is no set price for any of our dishes. You donate as much as you think it is worth. If you struggle to afford it but still want to help - do not worry! You can volunteer insted:
Your time and skills are very valuable to us. By helping us on the floor, in the kitchen or in the warehouse, you can get free meals or goods boxes. We want you to know there are many ways for you to access fresh food even when you struggle financially!

Food Waste

The annual food waste in the UK in 2018 was estimated at around 9.5 million tones worth around Â£19 billion a year (WRAP 2020). Only approx. 30% was inedible.
Many products from supermarkets are thrown away only because of their visual aspects, while still being good to eat.
In our restaurant we want to show that food waste does not always mean rotten, unusable or expired products. We use local distributors' food waste to prepare tasty, nutritional meals and make a good change.

Menu

Our menu changes everyday. We make sure to always have new exciting options. It includes a variety of dietary restrictions, such as: vegetarian, vegan, gluten free, nut free, etc.

Food Box

If you are not afraid to cook with varied ingredients, we have something for you! Everyday, we prepare fresh food boxes to collect from our warehouse. You can get them via subscription on pay as you feel basis. Payment with collection by cash or card.

Team

We are Harry and Megan. Both passionate about zero waste lifestyle and helping people. The idea of PAYF restaurant came from many conversations with our friends, students, young families and fellow zero waste activists. We want to be the good change in the world.

Mission

Why are we doing this?
There are many reasons:

  • Combatting food waste
  • Helping those in need
  • Promoting healthy lifestyle
  • Providing a safe space
  • Building a community
  • Supporting local businesses

We believe in changing the world one step at a time. By using diverted food we minimise the food waste of local supermarkets and distributors, giving a good example to other businesses. No set price means more accessibility for people with lower income and fighting the problem of hunger. We are aiming for providing a safe space where everyone can feel at home, get to know other people and be involved in the local community.
